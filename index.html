Density Functional Theory

Electronic properties of materials. Based on Quantum ESPRESSO, an integrated suite of open source computer codes for electronic-structure calculations and materials modeling at the nanoscale.

Molecular Modeling

Atomistic simulation of molecules and crystals. Based on LAMMPS, open-source molecular dynamics code for solid-state materials, biomolecules/polymers and coarse-grained or mesoscopic systems.

Fluid Dynamics

Macroscopic simulations of fluid flows. Based on OpenFOAM, an open source software package simulating complex flow patterns, turbulence, heat transfer, solid dynamics and electromagnetic processes

Intuitive User Interface

Built-in visualization and input data validation. Focus on results: do science, not computer science.

Realtime Status Tracking

Monitor key metrics of your simulation in real time. High performance and high throughput.

Scalable Computing

Point-click access to thousands of CPUs. Eliminate spendings on infrastructure and administration.
