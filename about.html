What is Exabyte.io?

A web-based state-of-the-art complete modeling and simulation environment. We let researchers develop new materials by predicting the relationships of a material's atomic and molecular structure with its properties and behavior. With no hardware to buy or software to install.

  • Our customers enjoy
  • Reduction in cost and time associated with physical testing and experimentation
  • Acceleration of the innovation process - developing new materials faster
  • Improved fundamental understanding of material properties and behavior
  • Powerful supercomputing capabilities in the cloud or installed on-premise
  • Automation, data aggregation and team collaboration with highest security standards
